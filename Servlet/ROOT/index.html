Why Investorgate?

Despite the government's hand-waving to the contrary, the stock market isn't a fair place. The reality is that instutional investors have a substantial advantages over everyday people. Hedge funds can shell out millions of dollars to access proprietary data on credit card transactions, satellite imagery, consumer browsing histories, social media sentiment, and much more. Professionals also enjoy easy access to corporate executives and industry experts. Retail investors simply cannot compete with the bottomless research budgets and expansive human networks of the professionals.

Fortunately, they don't have to. Instead, they can get all of Wall Street's time, effort, and money to work for them. By aggregating and analyzing Security & Exchange Commision disclosures, our site allows anyone to investigate and replicate the investment strategies of institutions. In fact, since investorgators imitate Wall Street's tactics without having to pay Wall Street's hefty fees, you may even outperform your favorite investors.
